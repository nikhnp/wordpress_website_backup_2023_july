Hosting

The success of a business’s website depends on the hosting provider. It’s basically a place where your website files are housed. Your hosting provider also controls your website’s accessibility and how quickly your website loads. For this reason, selecting a reliable, fast, and affordable hosting company is crucial.
There are certain things you should keep in mind while choosing a hosting provider.

A. Performance / Speed

Since hosting is what makes or breaks your website, you should keep in mind the performance and speed the hosting provider offers. While looking for hosting providers, look for ones that provide SSD storage as opposed to HDD storage.
Also, keep in mind the CPU limit and Storage space provided

B. Uptime

Uptime is the availability of your website expressed in percentage. Having the highest uptime is important as you always want your website to be online. You do not want to lose sales because your website was unavailable.
Uptime also reflects how confident the hosting provider is with their service. Some hosting providers provide compensation if the uptime falls below the quoted number.
Most hosting providers quote an uptime of ‘99.9%’.

C. Domain Emails

Domain emails are also crucial for most website owners. Domain emails allow you to have an email address from your domain name (example- contact@nikhilnepal.com.np).
When you send an email through a domain email, it looks more professional than contacting people through your personal email address. Consumers also trust a domain email more than a gmail or icloud account. Consumers positively associate communication through domain email. 
So, you need to keep in mind how many domain emails your plan has and if it will be enough for your use.

D. Backups

Look at how often your hosting provider offers backup for your website. Backups are important. If your website is having problems, having the ability to revert your website to the most recent backup is a must.
How frequently do you change the contents of your website? If you update your website every few months, you can choose hosting with monthly backups. But, if you update the website frequently, choose options with daily backups.

E. Support

Say your website is down. Or, your emails aren’t working. Getting hold of someone will save you a lot of time and frustration. So, looking at what type of support the hosting provider offers is important.

F. Account Limitations

Looking at what you aren’t provided is as important as knowing what you are provided with. You need to be sure of what you are getting and if it will be enough. So, compare what one hosting provider is providing with another so that you have absolute certainty that you are getting your money’s worth. 

G. Pricing

Pricing is one of the most important factors in choosing a hosting provider. However, the cheapest option doesn’t have as many features as the more pricey options. So, you should choose a balance of price and features.

H. Renewal Pricing (MORE IMPORTANT THAN PRICING)

When most hosting providers offer you the prices, they show you discounted pricing. That price will only be valid until your selected timeframe. When you renew the hosting, you will be subject to renewal pricing. So, also look at the renewal pricing while choosing your hosting provider.
The big, bold prices are more likely to be ‘introductory offers’.

Free Hosting

You can get free hosting for your website. However, free hosting providers have severe limitations. The most prominent limitation being speed. Free hosting also might not provide features like domain emails, backups, and support.

MY ADVICE

Hosting

I would advise you to use cheap hosting rather than using free hosting. Even the cheapest hosting is going to be leagues above free hosting plans.
If you want your website to rank higher in search, and if you want organic traffic, use paid hosting. Search engines assign scores to websites. Slower speed can give lower scores to your website.
If you are trying to host an ecommerce site, use paid hosting. If your website is static and most of the website visitors follow a link to reach your website, or something similar, you can use free hosting.
